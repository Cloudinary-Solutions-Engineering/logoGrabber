First-time setup

  1. Enter your Cloudinary cloud name.
  2. Enter your unsigned upload preset.
  3. Drag the generated bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar.
  4. Open a website and click the bookmarklet.

The tool does not ask for or send a separate upload folder. Configure any folder or delivery path behaviour directly in your Cloudinary upload preset.

What it finds

  • Inline SVG elements.
  • SVGs loaded via image, object, or embed tags.
  • SVG symbol and sprite references where accessible.
  • SVGs inside open shadow roots.

Asset names

Downloads and uploads are named from the root domain of the current website, followed by _logo. For example, www.example.com becomes example.com_logo.svg.

Candidate order

SVG candidates are sorted by rendered pixel size, largest first, to help push small icons and site furniture lower in the picker.
